Help & Tips

Interact with your AI assistant using voice or text.

  • Click the Microphone button to start speaking. Click again (or wait for silence) to stop.
  • Type your message in the input box and press Enter or click the Send () button.
  • Use the Settings button to adjust volume, speaking rate, language, and persona.
  • Toggle the AI Voice Output button ( / ) to enable/disable spoken responses.
  • Use the Mute button ( / ) to silence all audio.
  • Click Switch Character to go back and choose Deva or Devi.
  • Click Clear Chat to erase the current conversation.
  • Use the Theme button to cycle between Light, Dark, and Auto modes.

Example Prompts:

  • "What's the weather like in London?"
  • "Tell me a fun fact."
  • "Explain quantum physics simply."
